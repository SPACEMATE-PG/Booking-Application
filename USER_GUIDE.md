## üìÖ Booking a Property

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

#### 1. **Select Property**
   - Navigate to the property details page
   - Review all information carefully
   - Check available room types and their prices

#### 2. **Choose Room Type & Click "Book Now"**
   - Select your preferred room configuration (Single, Double, Triple sharing)
   - Note the monthly price and availability
   - Click **"Book Now"** button for that room type
   - You'll be redirected to the booking page

#### 3. **Enter Booking Details**

**Step 1: Select Room Type**
- Confirm or change your room selection from the dropdown
- View real-time availability and pricing

**Step 2: Choose Duration**
- Select booking period from dropdown:
  - 1 Month
  - 2 Months
  - 3 Months
  - 6 Months (Popular)
  - 12 Months (Best Value)

**Step 3: Pick Move-in Date**
- Click on the calendar button
- Select your move-in date (future dates only)
- See your selected date confirmation

**Step 4: Review Price Breakdown** (Real-time in sidebar)
- Monthly Rent: Based on selected room type
- Duration: Number of months selected
- Total Rent: Monthly rent √ó Duration
- Security Deposit: Equal to 1 month's rent (refundable)
- **Total Payable Now**: Shows amount based on payment method selected

#### 4. **Choose Payment Method**
   
   **Option A: Pay Full Amount** üí≥
   - Pay total rent (monthly rent √ó duration) + security deposit (1 month rent) now
   - Complete everything in one payment
   - Move in without additional payment
   - ‚úÖ No remaining balance
   
   **Option B: Pay Booking Amount** üí∞
   - Pay 20% booking amount now to secure the property
   - Pay remaining amount (rent + security deposit - booking amount) on move-in day
   - Flexible payment option
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è Remaining balance due on move-in

#### 5. **Confirm Booking**
   - Review all details in the sidebar
   - Click **"Confirm Booking"** button
   - Review booking summary in confirmation dialog:
     - Property name
     - Room type
     - Move-in date
     - Duration
     - Payment method
     - Total amount
   - Click **"Confirm & Pay"** to complete

#### 6. **Booking Confirmed!** ‚úÖ
   - Payment is processed immediately
   - Booking status: **"Confirmed"**
   - Transaction ID generated for reference
   - Success message displayed with transaction details
   - Automatically redirected to **"My Bookings"** page
   - Your booking appears under "Current Stays" in Dashboard

### Important Notes

- ‚úÖ **Instant Confirmation**: Booking and payment happen together
- üîí **Secure Payment**: All transactions are secure
- üìß **No Email Verification**: Quick and easy booking process
- üí∞ **Security Deposit**: Refundable at the end of your stay
- üì± **Transaction ID**: Auto-saved for your records
- üè† **Immediate Access**: View booking details right after confirmation
